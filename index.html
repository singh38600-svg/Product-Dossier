How the publication works

Each edition separates observation, interpretation, assumption and judgment. Important conclusions link to supporting evidence, limitations and confidence levels.

01 · Observe

Map the system

Company, product, users, regulation, competitors, incentives and failure modes.

02 · Challenge

Attack the thesis

Search for contradictory evidence, alternative explanations and unsupported leaps.

03 · Propose

Recommend experiments

Convert evidence into testable opportunities rather than confident-sounding prescriptions.

The goal is not to look intelligent by producing confident answers. It is to make it harder for the research to remain wrong.
