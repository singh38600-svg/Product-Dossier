
# Content and Design Guide

## Editorial voice

Use:
- calm, specific language
- evidence before conclusion
- visible uncertainty
- experiments rather than prescriptions
- “public evidence suggests” where appropriate

Avoid:
- “we know”
- exaggerated leadership claims
- job-seeking language
- unsupported numbers
- treating app reviews as representative
- promising business impact without an experiment

## Page hierarchy

The site uses a three-layer reading experience:

1. Homepage — establishes the publication and central thesis.
2. Edition page — gives the three-minute executive narrative.
3. Evidence and downloads — allow verification and deeper reading.

## Design principle

The design should disappear behind the clarity of the thinking.

Keep:
- generous whitespace
- restrained color
- serif headlines
- short sections
- visible confidence labels
- minimal animation

Avoid:
- dashboards for their own sake
- gradients
- excessive glass effects
- bright startup colors
- invented product screenshots
- decorative charts without real data
