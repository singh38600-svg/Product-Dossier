About the publication

Useful work before an introduction.

The Product Dossier is an independent research publication. It studies a company or product using public evidence, makes uncertainty visible and proposes experiments that may be useful to the people building it.

Purpose Non-goals Editorial standard Contact
Purpose

Create value that stands on its own.

This is not a résumé, portfolio or unsolicited consulting pitch. The work should remain useful even if no job opening exists.

Non-goals

No insider performance

The publication does not pretend to know internal roadmaps, metrics, incidents or effort estimates.

No hiring request

The work is shared because it may be useful, not as a disguised demand for an interview.

No financial advice

The edition analyzes a product and company. It does not recommend trading or investing.

No volume for its own sake

The long report preserves rigor; the website creates a clear doorway into it.

Editorial standard

Evidence before opinion.

Claims are linked to sources. Assumptions are labeled. Contradictions and rejected claims are preserved. Recommendations are framed as testable hypotheses.
